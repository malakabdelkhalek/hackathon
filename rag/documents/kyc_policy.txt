NORDA BANK — INTERNAL KYC POLICY DOCUMENT v2.3

RISK TIER DEFINITIONS:

LOW RISK:
- EU/EEA resident
- Salary or pension source of funds
- No PEP status confirmed
- No sanctioned countries in transaction history
- Standard retail account type

MEDIUM RISK:
- Non-EU resident OR business source of funds
- No PEP status
- Transactions to medium-risk jurisdictions
- Corporate account with transparent ownership structure

HIGH RISK:
- PEP status confirmed
- Source of funds from offshore company, investment portfolio, or unknown origin
- Transactions to high-risk jurisdictions: UAE, Malta, Cyprus, BVI
- Nationality from FATF grey-listed country
- Any sanctioned entity connection detected

ESCALATION RULES:
- LOW RISK: Auto-approve onboarding, standard monitoring
- MEDIUM RISK: Enhanced due diligence required, compliance officer review within 5 business days
- HIGH RISK: MANDATORY human approval before account creation, senior officer sign-off required
- SANCTIONED: Automatic rejection, mandatory report to authorities

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS BY TIER:
- LOW: National ID plus proof of address
- MEDIUM: ID plus proof of address plus source of funds declaration
- HIGH: All above plus enhanced background check plus senior officer written sign-off
